What Each Grade Means
🟢
Grade A — Best Setup
All 5 conditions line up. Real volume, real breakout, real momentum. Only trade these if you're busy.
🟡
Grade B — Decent Setup
3–4 conditions met. Good but not perfect. Skip if market signal is yellow or red.
🔴
Grade C — Skip It
Only 1–2 conditions met. Don't force it. Missing a bad trade IS a win.
The 5 Real Conditions We Check
📈
Trend Direction
Is the stock above its 50-day average? That means it's been going up consistently — trade with the trend.
📦
Volume Surge
Is today's volume higher than the 10-day average? Big moves on big volume = real conviction.
🎯
Near 52-Week High
Stocks breaking to new highs often keep running. Within 5% of the 52-week high is a strong signal.
💪
Momentum (RSI)
RSI 50–72 = strong but not overheated. The sweet spot for entering a trade with room to run.
🛡️
Positive Today
Is the stock up today? Confirms the market agrees with the setup right now.
🌡️
Market Signal
GREEN = trade A setups. YELLOW = 1 perfect trade max. RED = sit on your hands.
