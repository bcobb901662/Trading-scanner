The A+ Setup System
Every stock is scored on 5 conditions. Grade A = 4 or 5 conditions met. You only trade Grade A setups — no exceptions.
1
Uptrend vs 20d ago
2
Volume surge 1.3x+
3
5-day momentum >1%
4
RSI 50-72 sweet spot
5
Green candle today
⚠️ High IV Warning
When a stock moves 5%+ in a single day, options premiums become extremely expensive due to high implied volatility (IV). Even if you pick the right direction, you can still lose money because you overpaid for the option. The scanner flags these automatically — skip options on IV warning stocks.
📉 PUT Setups
PUT setups are the bearish version — you profit when the stock goes DOWN. The scanner now looks for: downtrend vs 20 days ago, high volume on a down day, momentum falling >1%, and RSI overextended low (28-48). On red market days, check the Puts tab first.
📅 Your Daily Routine
1
Before work (6-7am CST): Open scanner → check Market Mood banner. If RED, close the scanner and go to work. Done.
2
If GREEN or YELLOW: Look at Calls tab → find Grade A setups. Open TradingView 1H → confirm zone ✅ + RSI ✅ + triangle fired.
3
Before entering: Run the Pre-Trade Checklist. All 10 checked? Enter + set limit sell at +50% or +100% immediately.
4
During work: Stop-loss is set. Do not check it. Let it work.
5
After 3pm CST: Check results. If contract down 50% at any point → close, no second-guessing. Exit day trades by 2:30 PM CST.
